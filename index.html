About us

Mappiya is a Filipino-made app for safe, hassle-free deliveries and ordering your needs online without the need to go out. The company was established in the middle of the COVID-19 pandemic and served as a source of income to Filipinos who lost their jobs. The aim of this app is to connect connect Saudi Arabia Filipinos to the OFW through the use of this app.
